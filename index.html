Our Story

Education that goes beyond marks

Established on 25 October 2019, Black Board Tuition Classes helps students achieve academic goals through quality teaching, personalized guidance and structured learning programs.

In association with Vidya Home Tuition, we provide online education across India while continuing offline coaching and home tuition support. Our focus includes confidence, discipline, critical thinking and life skills.
